Nineteen nights in Japan for Michael, Brittany, and the two boys (7 and 4) —
and not a single car the whole way. Shinkansen between cities, local trains and
metro within them, the Hakone Freepass, buses, and Yamato luggage forwarding
sending the bags ahead to each next hotel.

## The Route

Four legs — Tokyo Bay, Hakone, Osaka, and back to Tokyo — flying home out of
Haneda.

### 🎢 Tokyo Bay · Disney — Jun 6–10

_Grand Nikko Tokyo Bay Maihama_

- The Sanno Matsuri festival
- A day at Tokyo DisneySea
- A day at Tokyo Disneyland

### ♨️ Hakone — Jun 10–13

_Rakuten STAY TERRACE Kowakudani_

- A private in-room hot spring
- The Hakone Open-Air Museum
- The Lake Ashi pirate ship, Hakone Shrine, and the ropeway loop

### 🐙 Osaka — Jun 13–20

_Ostay Vermillion Namba_

- The Osaka Aquarium
- A Dotonbori night cruise
- Fushimi Inari at dawn and the Kyoto sake district (day trip)
- Super Nintendo World at Universal Studios Japan, plus the Kirby Café
- The Sakai knife district
- A day trip to Kobe

### 🗼 Tokyo · Roppongi — Jun 20–24

_Hotel RIN Roppongi_

- The Pokémon Center Tokyo DX
- Michael's birthday — Shibuya Sky, an NPB baseball game, and a birthday dinner
- teamLab Borderless at Azabudai Hills
- REONA Sushi
- A sumo experience
- Kappabashi kitchen town (and a VG10 chef's knife)

## Highlights

19 nights, four legs, three theme parks, two day trips — and zero cars.
